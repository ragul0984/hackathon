About The Event

TECHNOVA'26 is a premium National Level Hackathon organized by the Department of Information Technology at Dhanalakshmi Srinivasan Engineering College (Autonomous), Perambalur.

Designed to be a crucible for bright minds across the nation, this 24-hour non-stop hackathon provides a platform for engineering students to design and build technology-driven, frugal innovations that solve critical challenges for a sustainable society.

We invite innovators, code wizards, and problem solvers to build scalable, low-cost prototypes that will drive our planet toward a greener, smarter, and more equitable future.

Open Innovation Format

Design Your Own Frugal Solution

TECHNOVA'26 operates under an Open Innovation model. We do not restrict you to predefined sub-categories. Instead, your team is invited to identify any real-world challenge related to environmental, agricultural, civic, or healthcare sustainability, and construct a frugal (low-cost, high-efficiency) hardware or software solution for it.

Timeline & Schedule

Roadmap of TECHNOVA'26 from submission to pitch day

AUG 11, 2026

Idea PPT Submission Deadline

Submit your team registration details and ideation PowerPoint slides (PPT) outlining the problem statement, frugal hardware/software architecture, and expected impact.

AUG 14, 2026

Selection Announcement

Idea submissions will be evaluated by an expert panel. Top teams will be notified via email for the physical round. Selection criteria includes frugality, impact, and feasibility.

AUG 20, 2026 (08:30 AM)

Hackathon Inauguration & Coding Start

Reporting at the DSEC IT department lab. Welcome address, mentor assignments, and release of final instructions. Code/Build starts at 9:30 AM sharp.

AUG 20, 2026 (09:00 PM)

Midnight Mentor Review

Experts and professors review progress of the running prototype. Direction adjustments, architectural optimizations, and troubleshooting support.

AUG 21, 2026 (08:30 AM)

Code Freeze & Pre-Evaluation

24-hour coding window shuts. Teams must stop development and upload codebase/design documents. Initial validation checks begin.

AUG 21, 2026 (11:00 AM)

Final Jury Presentation

Teams showcase running prototypes to the external judging panel. Standard format: 5 minutes demo + 3 minutes Q&A. Focus on sustainable feasibility.

AUG 21, 2026 (03:00 PM)

Valedictory & Prize Distribution

Announcement of winners. Awarding cash prizes from the unified prize pool, trophies, and distribution of participation certificates to all attending teams.
